what is

spectrum preschool?

Spectrum Preschool of Austin aims to EMPOWER families affected by Autism EARLY in the home-to-school transition. We provide research-based, early intervention special education services in a technology-rich environment to preschool aged children (2.5-5 yrs) diagnosed with Autism Spectum Disorder (ASD).

  • Research Benefits for Children
  • Programs for Educators
  • Dynamic Classrooms

Our staff of teachers are all ABA certified and/or are working towards certification in addition to receiving regular ABA trainings.

Technology



Our preschool utilizes interactive whiteboards combined with iPad touch-screen technology as a medium to communicate, a motivating centerpiece for social skill building, and an integral data tracking tool for parents, therapists, and teachers.
